What is Blockchain?

The answer is not simple, but it is also not complicated.
Blockchain is literally just a chain of blocks but not in the
traditional sense of those words. We are actually talking
about digital pieces of information - block - stored in a
public database - chain. It is a protocol designed for
storage and digital transactions in a safe and
non-corruptible way.
